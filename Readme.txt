ColtPay Commerce provides merchants with an easy way to accept Bitcoin payments on their WordPress WooCommerce website.

== Description ==

Accept Bitcoin on your website today. With a simple click of the mousepad or QR scan from your smartphone, you'll receive quick, simple BTC payments

With ColtPay you can accept Bitcoin as payment on your E-Commerce store and get paid within hours. All settlements are processed same day and directly linked to your E-Wallet or Bitcoin wallet

ColtPay lets you accept online payments from your eCommerce site, using the Transactions API and a customizable payment form. In addition, ColtPay APIs let you track your sales, orders, and inventory in one place.

The add-on is fully responsive and works perfectly with any theme, on any device to accept payments in your WooCommerce store.

== Installation ==
Install the ColtPay Commerce plugin in your WordPress admin by going to *Plugins* > *Add New* and searching for *'ColtPay'* or -if doing a manual install- download the plugin and unzip into your `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.

= Configuration =

Log into WordPress admin.  Click on WooCommerce->Settings->Payments Tab. You can see ColtPay has been added as a payment gateway option.  Click on Manage to configure settings.

Enter the ColtPay API key obtained from your merchant admin at coltpay.com.  It will be found under the API Reference item on the left sidebar.

The configuration is complete. Your customer will be able to checkout and pay using ColtPay (Bitcoin) option.
